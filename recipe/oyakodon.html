Oyakodon

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Onion
  • Sake
  • Eggs
  • Oyakodon simmer sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Thinly cut onions into strips. Cut Chicken into cubes.
  2. Pour simmer sauce into shallow pan. Add onions and simmer on medium heat until semi-soft.
  3. Add chicken and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Add eggs and simmer
  5. Serve mixture over hot rice.
