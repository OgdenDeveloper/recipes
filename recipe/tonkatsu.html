Tonkatsu

Ingredients

  • Lean Pork Cutlets
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Tonkatsu Sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Pound pork cutlets to thin even layer.
  2. Prepare breading ingredient by creating three separate bowls. One bowl flour with salt and pepper. The second bowl, beat an egg. The third bowl add panko bread crumb.
  3. Dredge cutlet in stages, first flour, then egg, finishing with panko crumb.
  4. Heat three cups of oil in a pot and bring to 340 degrees F.
  5. Fry the pork cutlet until safe internal temp
  6. Cut tonkatsu into strips and serve with cabbage and tonkatsu sauce.
