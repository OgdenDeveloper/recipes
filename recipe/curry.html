Curry

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Potato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken Breast
  • Curry Cube
  • Cooking oil
  • Chicken Broth

Cooking Steps

  1. Clean and cut all ingredients into uniform cubes about 1 inch
  2. Heat cooking oil in pot, add the onion and garlic cooking until soft.
  3. Add in chicken and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Add in chicken broth and simmer uncovered for 20 minutes
  5. Turn off heat and add in curry cube, stir until included and thickened.
  6. Serve over a bed of hot rice.
